Why ALS Progresses

Under cellular stress, neurons form stress granules to protect RNA. In ALS, these granules persist, promoting pathological aggregation of TDP-43 and accelerating neuronal death.

Our Approach

Granulyze employs a dual-strategy: antisense oligonucleotides to reduce stress granule formation and RNA aptamers to inhibit TDP-43 aggregation.
