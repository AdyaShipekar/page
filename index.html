The Biology Behind ALS

Under cellular stress, neurons form stress granules to protect RNA. In ALS, persistent granules seed pathological TDP-43 aggregation.

Cellular mechanism illustration

Our Dual-Layered Solution

Granulyze combines antisense oligonucleotides with RNA aptamers to disrupt toxic aggregation pathways.
